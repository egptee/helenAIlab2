我们关注的不是"AI 多强大"或"谁会被取代"。

我们关注的是——

当 AI 以 10 分钟完成传统需要 10 小时的合同审查，当 GPT-4 通过了律师资格考试，

法律人的选择能不能变得更多、更自由、更从容。


Lab 的存在，是为了提供一种新的可能：

一个能看清 Legal AI 趋势、遇见跨界伙伴、探索新职业方向的地方。

We're not here to hype AI or fear-monger about job displacement.

We're here to ask a more human question:


When AI can review contracts in 10 minutes (not 10 hours), when GPT-4 passes the bar exam,

how can lawyers navigate this shift with more freedom—not less?


The Lab exists to offer clarity on Legal AI trends, connections with cross-disciplinary thinkers,

and pathways to new career possibilities.

为什么会有这个 Lab Why This Lab Exists

因为法律行业的变化史无前例，

而大多数律师仍在观望，缺少真正的对话和指引。


因为法律人习惯了独自钻研判例，

但 AI 时代的机会属于那些懂得跨界连接的人。


因为我们相信：

理解 Legal AI，不应该比它改变行业的速度更慢。

Because the legal industry is changing at an unprecedented pace,

yet most lawyers are still waiting and watching, without real guidance or conversation.


Because lawyers are trained to work solo through case law,

but AI-era opportunities belong to those who connect across disciplines.


Because we believe:

Understanding Legal AI shouldn't lag behind the speed at which it's transforming the profession.

Founder · Legal AI Observer · Cross-Disciplinary Connector

Helen Fan 是一位驻硅谷的律师及法律科技战略家，毕业于哥伦比亚大学法学院。她拥有六年跨越政府机构、跨国公司和顶级律所的执业经验，对法律系统的运作有着多层次的理解——从监管到企业决策，再到客户服务。

她在法律与科技的交汇点创立了这个 Legal AI Lab，帮助法律人应对向 AI 原生、数据驱动的工作流程转型。她的使命很简单：让从业者、运营者和创新者能够参与——而不仅仅是旁观——法律行业的 AI 变革。

Helen Fan is a Silicon Valley–based lawyer and LegalTech strategist trained at Columbia Law School. With six years of experience across government agencies, global companies, and leading law firms, she brings a multilayered understanding of how legal systems operate — from regulation to corporate decision-making to client service.

Working at the intersection of law and technology, she founded this Legal AI Lab to help legal professionals navigate the shift toward AI-native, data-driven workflows. Her mission is simple: empower practitioners, operators, and innovators to participate — not just spectate — in the AI transformation of the legal profession.
